Memento Pattern
This pattern capture the internal state of the object in a memento, allowing
the object to restore its state later on without revealing internal details.

Example Undo and Redo fucntionality
Interview - Suppose you're designing a document editor.

Components
-Originator: The object whos states needs to be saved and restore (TextEditor)
-Memento: Captures and stores the internal state of the Originator (EditorMemento)
-Caretaker: Manages and stores the memento's, without modifying them (Caretaker)

UML Diagram

TextEditor - - - -> EditorMemento ────♦ Caretaker

TextEditor dependence on EditorMemento to create & save a snapshot or undo & restore snapshot
Caretaker manages snapshots of the EditorMemento, without Caretaker there are no snapshots to manage
